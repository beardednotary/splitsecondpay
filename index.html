3 Taps. Done. The Fastest Split at the Table.

We respect your time. Here is the entire process for splitting any standard bill with SplitSecond Pay.

  1. Tap 1: Enter the Bill Total Open the app and simply type the final subtotal. Tax is added automatically, and you can instantly adjust your preset tip percentage (e.g., to 20%).
  2. Tap 2: Choose How Many Ways Select the number of people splitting the bill with a single tap. The per-person amount updates instantly in large, easy-to-read numbers.
  3. Tap 3: Send Payment Request Use the free Share via Text summary, or go Pro to unlock direct Venmo or PayPal deep links that pre-fill the exact amount for instant payment.

FAQs: Quick Answers Before You Download

We built SplitSecond Pay to solve common pain points. Here are the most common questions we hear.

What is SplitSecond Pay best for?

It’s optimized for group dining, bars, and cafés. The simple one-screen flow is tuned for the moment the paper check lands, but it works for any expense you need to split quickly.

Does every person need to download the app?

No. Only the person running the calculation needs the app. You calculate the share, then request payment from your friends using any method you prefer (Venmo, PayPal, Zelle, or cash).

Do you collect my data or require an account?

Absolutely not. SplitSecond Pay is privacy-first. We require no sign-up, no email, no password, and collect no usage data or analytics. All calculations stay on your device.

How is this app free? What's the business model?

The core, 3-tap splitting app is 100% free and has no ads. We offer SplitSecond Pay Pro as an optional one-time upgrade for users who want advanced features like direct Venmo/PayPal deep links, OCR Receipt Scanning, and unlimited history tracking.
